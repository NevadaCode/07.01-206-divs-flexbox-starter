Hi. I'm Nevada

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who thrives on visual storytelling and the best user experience. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects or researching new skills to learn, I can be found traveling, hiking and cooking away in my kitchen.

Where it all started: I was born in Cape Town, South Africa, but moved to Johannesburg as a teenager. My mom always pushed my dream of moving to Europe so I travelled a lot and finally planted some roots in Edinburgh, Scotland. I love this wee city, but the road will take me where it must!

My passion: I love cooking and eating! Trying a new texture, tasting a new flavour and smelling the aroma of something exciting brings pure joy and happiness into my life. Sitting around a table to feast with loved ones is what life is truly about, paired with a good whiskey or wine is just the cherry on top.

Fact: I still need to get a drivers licence! When I lived in Johannesburg I drove a little Suzuki 125cc scooter because I couldn't be one of the many people stuck in traffic for hours. Now that I live in Edinburgh I rely on public transport which is better for the environment!
